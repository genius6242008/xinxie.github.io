Hi, I'm Xin Xie. I conduct research on the psychology of language.

I am currently a post-doctoral researcher in the lab of Florian Jaeger in the Department of Brain and Cognitive Sciences at University of Rochester.

Previously, I obtained my Ph.D. in Experimental Psychology from University of Connecticut, under the mentorship of Emily Myers and Carol Fowler. I then completed a one-year postdoc training in the Language and Brain Lab.
